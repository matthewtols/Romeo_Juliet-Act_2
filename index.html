Act 2

Scene 1

this scene begins with romeo jumping over the wall of capulet's orchard. the view then shifts to benvolio and mercutio who see romeo jump the fence and assume he went to see Rosaline. they decide following him would be worthless and they leave.

Scene 2

after romeo jumps the wall into capulet's orchard, he goes to juliet's window, she comes out and they talk about their love. juliet tells romeo to send word of wether he wants to marry her tomorrow.

Scene 3

romeo goes to friar Lawrence's cell. friar lawrence finds out that romeo is no longer in love with rosaline. romeo asks friar lawrence to marry him and juliet. friar lawrence agrees to marry romeo and juliet.

Scene 4

at the start of this scene, benvolio and mercutio discuss a challenge sent to romeo from tybalt. romeo enters and they talk for a little bit. nurse and peter enter and mercutio starts to insult Nurse's age. romeo sends mercutio and benvolio away. romeo asks nurse to tell juliet to go to friar lawrence's cell to marry him.

Scene 5

juliet is wondering where nurse is when nurse and peter arrive. juliet tells nurse to send peter away. juliet is eager for the news of romeo and after catching her breath, nurse tells juliet to go to friat lawrence's cell to marry romeo.

Scene 6

romeo and juliet meet in friar lawrence's cell and friar lawrence marries them.
